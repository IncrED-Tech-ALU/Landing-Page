About Us

Welcome to our think tank, where we are dedicated to enhancing learning experiences and increasing education accessibility through creative technology. Our mission is to equip learners in different environments with the knowledge to tackle global challenges and forge a better Africa through quality education. At the core of our brand are the values of innovation, creativity, empathy, and passion. We believe in creating people-centric, innovative, and creative solutions that address the challenges faced by learners in accessing quality education materials. We understand that Rwandan students need help accessing quality learning materials that are engaging and user-friendly.

According to Rwanda Today (2022), over 40% of students in Rwanda are dissatisfied with the current educational materials, which hampers their academic progress and potential. This is why our think tank focuses on solving this issue by providing accessible and innovative educational solutions to empower learners to achieve their full potential and contribute to building a better Africa through education. We believe technology can be a powerful force for good, and we are using it to create simple, creative, accessible, and affordable learning materials in the form of cards. We designed these cards to help teachers "learn faster and teach better," making learning more engaging and effective for learners.

We are on a mission to make a difference in the lives of learners and educators in Rwanda and beyond. We invite you to join us on this journey, to be a part of something bigger than ourselves, and to help us build a better Africa through quality education. Together, we can make a difference and create a brighter future for future generations.

Our Solution

IncrED Learning cards

Stem resources

IncrED Learning Cards

SDG-4 resouces

Our solution to address the digital divide and language barrier is to create concise and creative learning cards in local languages for teachers to "learn faster and teach better." Teachers can easily access these offline cards in areas with limited or no access to digital devices or internet connectivity. By leveraging an A.I. model, we will input all the relevant information, including exam questions and answers, to create comprehensive topic summaries that can be condensed into pocket-sized cards. This approach will enable teachers to quickly and easily access all the necessary information and teach effectively.
